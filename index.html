INFO

Severling is a dark electronic audio-visual project by musician Joseph Beaty, combining glitch-driven music, ambient layers, experimental sound design, and visual corruption art. Blending fractured rhythms, degraded textures, and immersive atmospheres, the project explores themes of memory, decay, dreams, and digital erosion through both sound and image.
